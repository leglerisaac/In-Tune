Choir Members

  • High School age youth.
  • Auditioned Youth, not for beginners.
  • Primarily members of the Church Of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Exceptions will be reviewed case by case if needed.

Rehearsals

  • Rehearsals will be on Sunday evenings from 7-9pm (September through May).
  • We will hold rehearsals in church chapels. Locations will vary but will try to be close to the 205 corridor in Oregon or Washington.
  • There will be a dress standard at rehearsals.

Auditions

  • Auditions will be held in late May and early June.
  • Go to "Audition Sign-Up" to sign up.

Dues/Payments

  • There will be a cost of $200 per youth for administrative costs, music, insurance, fall retreat, etc.
  • There will be additional costs for uniforms (suits and dresses) and a small retreat fee.

Yearly Activities

  • Retreats: One in the fall and possibly an optional one in the late spring.
  • The Fall overnight retreat will be held on September 19th & 20th.
  • Fall Saturday workshop
  • Winter Saturday workshop
  • We will also have a few social events to provide an opportunity for the choir members to have some fun and socialize with eachother. These may be an additional minimal costâ€”dates/times TBD.

Performances

  • During performances we will be singing Christ centered music based on a theme and they will include spoken messages, thoughts or testimonies.
  • In December we will schedule 3-4 performances which will be primarily on Sundays or an occasional Saturday night.
  • There will be 3-4 fireside performances in the Spring on Sundays.

Performance Attire

  • Suits and a tie will be $125
  • Dresses TBD (not to exceed $100)

Communication

  • The In tune Youth Choir will be using a communication app for both its members and parents. This is where we will send youth and parents important information regarding rehearsals, performances and retreats.

Volunteers

  • Parents volunteers are so important to the In Tune Youth Choir. We need you!
  • Please fill out parent form at the time of your youths audition. We will have forms available.
